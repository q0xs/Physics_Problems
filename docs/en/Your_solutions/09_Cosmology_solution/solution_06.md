# Task 09.06 – Time Until Andromeda Collision

## Problem Statement

Andromeda is $2.5$ million light-years away and approaches at $110\text{ km/s}$. Estimate the collision time at constant speed.

## Theory

Use distance divided by speed after converting light-years to kilometers.

$$
t=\frac{d}{v}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
d=2.5\times10^6(9.461\times10^{12})=2.37\times10^{19}\text{ km}
$$

$$
t=\frac{2.37\times10^{19}}{110}=2.15\times10^{17}\text{ s}
$$

$$
t=6.82\times10^9\text{ yr}
$$

## Final Result

$$
t\approx6.8\text{ billion years}
$$

## Interpretation

The estimate is simplified because gravity and transverse motion affect the real future encounter.
