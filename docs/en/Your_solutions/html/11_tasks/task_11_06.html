Task 11.06 · Quantum Numbers for n Equals 3

For n=3, list possible l and m_l values and count distinct electron states.

Live Controls

Simulation Concept

This page uses a quantum-scale model of waves, levels, photons, decay products, or probability because the task is Quantum Numbers for n Equals 3.

Formula Focus

l=0,1,ldots,n-1
m_l=-l,-l+1,ldots,l
1+3+5=9 orbital states

Final Result

There are 9 orbital states, or 18 electron states when spin is included.

Physical Interpretation

The number of orbital states in shell n is n^2, and including spin gives 2n^2.
