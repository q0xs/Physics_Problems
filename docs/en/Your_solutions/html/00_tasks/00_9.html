The Optimization Balance

As the width $x$ increases, the vertex $(x, y)$ moves down the parabola, decreasing the height. The maximum area occurs where the rate of increase in width is exactly countered by the rate of decrease in height. Here, the optimal corner is at $(1, 2)$.
