# Task 06.07 – Capacitors in Parallel

## Problem Statement

Two capacitors $C_1=4\,\mu\text{F}$ and $C_2=6\,\mu\text{F}$ are connected in parallel to a $10\text{ V}$ battery. Find the total stored charge and total energy.

## Theory

Parallel capacitors share the same voltage and their capacitances add.

$$
C_{\text{eq}}=C_1+C_2
$$

$$
Q=C_{\text{eq}}V
$$

$$
W=\frac{1}{2}C_{\text{eq}}V^2
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
C_{\text{eq}}=4\,\mu\text{F}+6\,\mu\text{F}=10\,\mu\text{F}
$$

$$
Q=(10\times10^{-6})(10)=1.00\times10^{-4}\text{ C}
$$

$$
W=\frac{1}{2}(10\times10^{-6})(10)^2=5.0\times10^{-4}\text{ J}
$$

## Final Result

$$
Q=100\,\mu\text{C}
$$

$$
W=5.0\times10^{-4}\text{ J}
$$

## Interpretation

Parallel connection increases the plate area effectively available for storing charge, increasing total capacitance.
