# Task 10.05 – Relativistic Mass at 99.9 Percent Light Speed

## Problem Statement

Find the historical relativistic mass of a $50\text{ kg}$ person moving at $99.9\%$ of the speed of light.

## Theory

Modern treatments prefer invariant rest mass and total energy, but the historical relativistic mass is $m_{rel}=\gamma m$.

$$
m_{rel}=\gamma m
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
\gamma=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-0.999^2}}=22.37
$$

$$
m_{rel}=(22.37)(50)=1.12\times10^3\text{ kg}
$$

## Final Result

$$
m_{rel}=1.12\times10^3\text{ kg}
$$

## Interpretation

The result reflects increased total energy, not a change in the object's invariant rest mass.
