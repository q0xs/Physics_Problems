Momentum Conservation

In a horizontal collision where external forces (like friction) are neglected during the short interval of the jump, total momentum is conserved.

\[m_1v_1 + m_2v_2 = (m_1 + m_2)v_f\]

Since the cart is initially stationary (\(v_2 = 0\)):

\[v_f = \frac{m_1v_1}{m_1 + m_2}\]

Energy Non-Conservation

In a perfectly inelastic collision (where objects stick together), kinetic energy is always lost. The energy is converted into sound, heat, and internal work to lock the bodies together.

\[\Delta K = K_f - K_i = \frac{1}{2}(m_1+m_2)v_f^2 - \frac{1}{2}m_1v_1^2\]
