# Task 05 – Echo Ranging

## Problem Statement

An echo is heard after 1 s. Determine the distance to the cliff.

## Theory

The sound travels to the cliff and back:

$$
d = \frac{vt}{2}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
d = \frac{343 \times 1}{2}
$$

$$
d = 171.5 \, \text{m}
$$

## Final Result

$$
d = 171.5 \, \text{m}
$$

## Interpretation

The division by 2 accounts for the round-trip travel of sound.
