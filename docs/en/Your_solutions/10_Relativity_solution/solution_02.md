# Task 10.02 – Time Dilation by a Factor of Two

## Problem Statement

How fast must a spaceship move for an Earth device to play two one-hour episodes while the spaceship device plays one?

## Theory

Time dilation means the Earth observer sees the moving clock run slow by the Lorentz factor.

$$
\Delta t=\gamma\Delta\tau
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
\gamma=2
$$

$$
2=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-v^2/c^2}}
$$

$$
v=0.866c
$$

## Final Result

$$
v=0.866c
$$

## Interpretation

The moving device experiences one hour of proper time while two hours pass in the Earth frame.
