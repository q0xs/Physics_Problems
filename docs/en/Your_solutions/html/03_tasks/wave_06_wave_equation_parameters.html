Step-by-Step Physics

General form

A traveling wave can be written as $y(x,t)=A\sin(kx-\omega t)$.

Parameter relations

$k = \frac{2\pi}{\lambda}$, $\omega = 2\pi f$, and $v = \frac{\omega}{k}$.

Interpretation

The coefficient of $x$ controls spatial repetition, while the coefficient of $t$ controls temporal repetition.
