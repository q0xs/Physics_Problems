Step-by-Step Physics

Formula

The phase difference between two points separated by $\Delta x$ is $\Delta\phi = \frac{2\pi}{\lambda}\Delta x$.

Normalized form

If the separation is written as a fraction of a wavelength, $\Delta x = r\lambda$, then $\Delta\phi = 2\pi r$.

Interpretation

A full wavelength corresponds to $2\pi$ radians. Fractions of a wavelength produce proportionally smaller phase shifts.
