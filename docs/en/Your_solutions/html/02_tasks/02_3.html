Energy Conservation

When the bob is released, it converts its initial Gravitational Potential Energy (\(U\)) into Kinetic Energy (\(K\)) as it descends.

\[mgh = \frac{1}{2}mv^2\]

Solving for velocity \(v\):

\[v = \sqrt{2gh}\]

Geometry of the Drop

The vertical height \(h\) depends on the length \(L\) and the angle \(\theta\):

\[h = L - L\cos\theta = L(1 - \cos\theta)\]
