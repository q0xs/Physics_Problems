Task 07.04 · Relative Uncertainty of a Speedometer

A car speedometer has 5% uncertainty. If it reads 60 km/h, find the possible range of actual speed.

Live Controls

Simulation Concept

This page uses a trajectory animation that follows the motion equation and compares key flight quantities because the task is Relative Uncertainty of a Speedometer.

Formula Focus

Delta v = left((p)/(100)right)v
Delta v=0.05(60)=3 km/h
v_{min}=60-3=57 km/h

Final Result

The actual speed range is 57 km/h to 63 km/h.

Physical Interpretation

The instrument reading gives a central estimate, not an exact value.
