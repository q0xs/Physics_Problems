Mathematical Derivation

Step 1: $\omega$

Calculate angular speed from period $T$. $\omega = \frac{2\pi}{86400} \approx 7.272 \times 10^{-5} \text{ rad/s}$.

Step 2: $a_c$

Apply $a_c = \omega^2 r$ using $r = 6,378,000 \text{ m}$. Resulting in $0.0337 \text{ m/s}^2$.

Conclusion
