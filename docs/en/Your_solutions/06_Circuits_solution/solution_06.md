# Task 06.06 – Ammeter Current in a Multi-Source Circuit

## Problem Statement

Calculate the current flowing through the ammeter in the second Kirchhoff circuit.

## Theory

The ammeter is in series with the $20\,\Omega$ branch, so its reading equals the current through that resistor. Nodal analysis is the most direct method for the two-source network.

$$
I_A = \frac{V_L-V_R}{R_2}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

Taking the source polarities as shown in the diagram and solving the two-node system gives

$$
V_R=-4.66\text{ V}\quad\text{relative to the left node}
$$

$$
I_A=\frac{0-(-4.66)}{20}=0.233\text{ A}
$$

## Final Result

$$
I_A=0.233\text{ A}
$$

## Interpretation

The sign convention indicates the current direction through the ammeter. The magnitude is the quantity read by an ideal ammeter.
