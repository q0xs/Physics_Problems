# Task 06 – Wave Equation

## Problem Statement

Given:

$$
y(x,t) = 0.05 \sin(2\pi x - 50\pi t)
$$

Find amplitude, wavelength, frequency, and wave speed.

## Theory

General form:

$$
y = A \sin(kx - \omega t)
$$

Relations:

$$
k = \frac{2\pi}{\lambda}, \quad \omega = 2\pi f, \quad v = \frac{\omega}{k}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

### Amplitude

$$
A = 0.05 \, \text{m}
$$

### Wave number

$$
k = 2\pi
$$

$$
\lambda = \frac{2\pi}{k} = 1 \, \text{m}
$$

### Angular frequency

$$
\omega = 50\pi
$$

$$
f = \frac{\omega}{2\pi} = 25 \, \text{Hz}
$$

### Wave speed

$$
v = \frac{\omega}{k} = \frac{50\pi}{2\pi} = 25 \, \text{m/s}
$$

## Final Result

- $A = 0.05 \, \text{m}$
- $\lambda = 1 \, \text{m}$
- $f = 25 \, \text{Hz}$
- $v = 25 \, \text{m/s}$

## Interpretation

The wave travels in the positive x-direction with moderate speed and small amplitude.
