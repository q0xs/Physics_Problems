# Task 07.06 – Digital Thermometer Precision

## Problem Statement

A digital thermometer reads $25.4^\circ\text{C}$. Assuming uncertainty is half the last digit, find the absolute uncertainty.

## Theory

The last displayed digit is $0.1^\circ\text{C}$, so half a digit is $0.05^\circ\text{C}$.

$$
\Delta T=\frac{0.1^\circ\text{C}}{2}=0.05^\circ\text{C}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

The reading should be written with its instrumental uncertainty as

$$
T=(25.4\pm0.05)^\circ\text{C}
$$

## Final Result

$$
\Delta T=0.05^\circ\text{C}
$$

## Interpretation

Digital precision is not the same as perfect accuracy; it gives a lower limit for reporting uncertainty.
