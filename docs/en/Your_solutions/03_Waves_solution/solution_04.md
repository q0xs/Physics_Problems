# Task 04 – Phase Difference

## Problem Statement

Find the phase difference between two points separated by $\lambda/3$.

## Theory

Phase difference:

$$
\Delta \phi = \frac{2\pi}{\lambda} \Delta x
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
\Delta x = \frac{\lambda}{3}
$$

$$
\Delta \phi = \frac{2\pi}{\lambda} \cdot \frac{\lambda}{3}
$$

$$
\Delta \phi = \frac{2\pi}{3}
$$

## Final Result

$$
\Delta \phi = \frac{2\pi}{3} \, \text{rad}
$$

## Interpretation

A separation of $\lambda/3$ corresponds to a phase shift of 120°.
