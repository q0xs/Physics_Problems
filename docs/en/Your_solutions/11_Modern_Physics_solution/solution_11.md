# Task 11.11 – Alpha Decay of Uranium-238

## Problem Statement

Give a balanced alpha decay equation starting with $^{238}_{92}\text{U}$.

## Theory

Alpha decay emits a helium nucleus, reducing mass number by $4$ and atomic number by $2$.

$$
{}^{A}_{Z}X\rightarrow{}^{A-4}_{Z-2}Y+{}^{4}_{2}\text{He}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
{}^{238}_{92}\text{U}\rightarrow{}^{234}_{90}\text{Th}+{}^{4}_{2}\text{He}
$$

## Final Result

$$
{}^{238}_{92}\text{U}\rightarrow{}^{234}_{90}\text{Th}+{}^{4}_{2}\text{He}
$$

## Interpretation

Both mass number and charge are conserved across the nuclear equation.
