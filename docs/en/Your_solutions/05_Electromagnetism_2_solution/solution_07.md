# Task 05.07 – Wavelength and Frequency of Green Light

## Problem Statement

The human eye is most sensitive to light with wavelength about $550\text{ nm}$. Identify the color and calculate the frequency.

## Theory

Frequency and wavelength of light in vacuum are related by $c=f\lambda$. A wavelength near $550\text{ nm}$ lies in the green part of the visible spectrum.

$$
f = \frac{c}{\lambda}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

Convert the wavelength:

$$
550\text{ nm}=5.50\times10^{-7}\text{ m}
$$

$$
f = \frac{3.00\times10^8}{5.50\times10^{-7}}
$$

$$
f = 5.45\times10^{14}\text{ Hz}
$$

## Final Result

$$
f = 5.45\times10^{14}\text{ Hz}
$$

## Interpretation

The color is green. The high frequency is typical of visible light, whose wavelengths are hundreds of nanometers.
