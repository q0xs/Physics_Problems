# Task 05.10 – Speed of Light in Diamond

## Problem Statement

Find the speed of light in diamond, which has refractive index $n=2.42$.

## Theory

The refractive index is the ratio of the speed of light in vacuum to the speed of light in the medium.

$$
n = \frac{c}{v}
$$

$$
v = \frac{c}{n}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
v = \frac{3.00\times10^8}{2.42}
$$

$$
v = 1.24\times10^8\text{ m/s}
$$

## Final Result

$$
v = 1.24\times10^8\text{ m/s}
$$

## Interpretation

Light travels more slowly in diamond than in vacuum because the electromagnetic wave interacts with the material.
