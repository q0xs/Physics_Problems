Theory

The period of a simple pendulum is:

T = 2π √(L / g)

Thus, the period depends on gravity as:

T ∝ 1 / √g

Solution

1. Moon Period

TMoon = TEarth × √(gEarth / gMoon)

For 4s on Earth, TMoon ≈ 9.80s

2. Length for 1s on Earth

L = g × (T / 2π)²

For 1s period, L ≈ 0.248m
