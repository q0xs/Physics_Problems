Superposition Field

Model

Each source at position $\vec r_0$ contributes

$u(\vec r,t)=\frac{A}{|\vec r-\vec r_0|^\alpha}\sin\big(k|\vec r-\vec r_0|-\omega t\big)$.

The total field is the sum over all sources. Increasing $\alpha$ strengthens radial attenuation. Setting $\alpha=0$ removes distance decay.
