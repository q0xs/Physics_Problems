# Task 07.05 – Percentage Uncertainty in Time

## Problem Statement

A time is recorded as $t=5.45\pm0.22\text{ s}$. Find the percentage uncertainty.

## Theory

Percentage uncertainty is the absolute uncertainty divided by the measured value, multiplied by $100\%$.

$$
p=\frac{\Delta t}{t}\times100\%
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
p=\frac{0.22}{5.45}\times100\%=4.04\%
$$

## Final Result

$$
p=4.04\%
$$

## Interpretation

This percentage expresses the uncertainty relative to the size of the measured time.
