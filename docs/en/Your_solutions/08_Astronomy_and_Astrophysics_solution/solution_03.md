# Task 08.03 – Microgravity at ISS Altitude

## Problem Statement

Find gravitational acceleration at $400\text{ km}$ altitude and explain why astronauts feel weightless.

## Theory

Gravity decreases with the square of distance from Earth's center, but it remains strong at low-Earth orbit.

$$
g=\frac{GM}{r^2}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
r=6.778\times10^6\text{ m}
$$

$$
g=\frac{(6.674\times10^{-11})(5.97\times10^{24})}{(6.778\times10^6)^2}=8.69\text{ m/s}^2
$$

## Final Result

$$
g=8.69\text{ m/s}^2
$$

## Interpretation

Astronauts feel weightless because they and the spacecraft are in continuous free fall around Earth, not because gravity is absent.
