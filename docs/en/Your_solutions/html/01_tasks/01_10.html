Numerical integration of $\int_{0}^{t_0} \sqrt{a^2\omega^2 \sin^2(\omega t) + b^2\omega^2 \cos^2(\omega t) + b^2} dt$

Special Case Check

a ≠ b: General Elliptic Helix

x = a·cos(ωt) y = b·sin(ωt) z = b·t

Geometric Interpretation

The point is constrained to the surface of an elliptic cylinder. As $t$ increases, the point rotates in the $xy$-plane while ascending linearly in $z$. The "pitch" of the helix is determined by the ratio of $b$ to $\omega$.

Velocity Components

$\vec{v} = (-a\omega\sin\omega t, b\omega\cos\omega t, b)$. Unlike a circular helix, the speed $|\vec{v}|$ of an elliptic helix is not constant over time unless $a = b$.
