Geometric Interpretation

The sine function forms a perfect arch between $0$ and $\pi$. Since the entire arch lies above the x-axis ($f(x) \geq 0$), the definite integral corresponds exactly to the physical area. Interestingly, this curved shape yields a perfectly integer area of exactly $2$.
